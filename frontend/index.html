Diamonds classification

Cut
The quality of the cut, from Fair to Ideal.
Color
The color of the diamond, from J to D.
Clarity
A measurement of how clear the diamond is, from I1 to IF.
Price
The price of the diamond, in US dollars.
X
The length of the diamond, in mm.
Y
The width of the diamond, in mm.
Z
The depth of the diamond, in mm.
Depth
The total depth percentage, measured as z / mean(x, y).
Table
The width of top of diamond relative to widest point.
